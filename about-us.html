The History of Wheel Hub

Explore the journey of our founding, our milestones in the automotive industry, the awards we've received, and the evolution of our brand.

On June 15, 1995, Wheel Hub, a pioneer in automotive retail, was founded in Los Angeles, California. Wheel Hub was established with a mission to provide quality vehicles and exceptional customer service.

Wheel Hubâ€™s journey began with the opening of its first showroom, which quickly became a hub for car enthusiasts. Over the years, we expanded our presence nationwide, offering a wide range of vehicles from various manufacturers. In 2001, we introduced our first online platform, setting a new standard in the automotive shopping experience.

Our commitment to innovation and excellence has earned us numerous awards, including the Automotive Retailer of the Year. We take pride in our dedicated team and loyal customers who have been with us throughout this journey.
